How HealthGuard AI Works

1. You describe what you feel

You enter simple details – age, location, and symptoms in your own words or voice.

2. AI generates a risk estimate

Your symptom description is sent securely to an AI model that estimates risk for key conditions.

3. You decide next steps

You can save or download your results and, in the next steps of the product, consult a doctor from the app.
